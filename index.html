Great Deals, But At What Cost?

51% of shoppers choose retailers based on discounts, with 30% off being the average for 2024. Merchants face the pressure to slash prices while balancing profit margins. Offering the best deal is essential, but without the right strategy, discounts can eat into profitability.

Multi-Channel Tracking Chaos

53% of merchants report that managing sales across multiple channels, from Meta, TikTok, Google Ads to marketplaces, is a major pain point. With so many platforms driving traffic, it's nearly impossible to pinpoint where the actual sales are coming from.

Stockouts and Delays Ruin the Sale

Fulfillment teams are overwhelmed by order volumes, leading to shipping delays and potentially disappointing customers. Merchants need automated solutions that ensure accurate stock management and fulfillment even during peak times.
